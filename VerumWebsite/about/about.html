The world of Verum, is a realm of magic, intrigue, conflict, and history. It is a place where nothing is certain and anything is possible. The setting consists of 24 planets, the main one, being the planet Verum, contains 7 continents with Kalkatesh being the one currently in focus.

Kalkatesh is a land that is steeped in a history of war and catastrophe, the greatest of which, The Reckoning nearly destroyed all of creation, before the intervention of the Seven. A band of mortals that rose up and put a stop to the greatest threat creation had ever known. Sixty-four years later, the land left behind struggles to recover from its wounds and old secrets begin to bubble to the surface, new struggles emerge and old threats return. What role will you play in the 6th Era?

Verum is currently hosting a living world, an MMO DnD experience, with 250 active players and a DM team over 50 strong. Many of its games are streamed and all games affect the same shared world in a multitude of ways. Please visit our discord to discover our 24/7 text RP and get involved as a DM or a player!

Arcadum is a Twitch streamer, professional Dungeon Master, and the creator and main Dungeon Master of the living world of Verum. Arcadum has been running games for over a decade professionally and has found great success streaming his games on his Twitch channel amassing over 200k followers. He currently runs five Dungeons & Dragons campaigns as well as a cyberpunk roleplay server in VR Chat called Callous Row.
